Who We Are

About Mekano Engineering

Established in 2006, Mekano Engineering Private Limited is a leading trader, wholesaler, retailer, distributor, and supplier of Fire Fighting & Safety Equipment, Industrial Valves, and Pressure Valves. Based in the heart of Chandni Chowk, New Delhi, we serve industries nationwide.

Our products are sourced from trusted manufacturers and pass stringent quality checks before delivery. Under Aman Bakshi's leadership, we have built an impeccable reputation for quality, reliability, and on-time delivery across sugar, chemical, paper, steel, cement, and engineering industries.
