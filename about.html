Who We Are

Mekano Engineering Pvt. Ltd.

Founded in 2006 and headquartered in Mumbai, Mekano Engineering Pvt. Ltd. has grown into one of Western India's most trusted industrial supply companies.

We are authorized dealers and distributors for some of India's most respected industrial brands including Shenco Valves, Indef Chain Pulleys, FESTO Pneumatics, L&T Valves, Audco Valves, Sant Valves, and Diamond Chains.

Our product portfolio covers 10 major industrial categories — from valve automation and pneumatic solutions to electric motors, gearboxes, and power transmission equipment. We serve industries including petrochemical, pharmaceutical, food processing, textile, construction, and general manufacturing.
